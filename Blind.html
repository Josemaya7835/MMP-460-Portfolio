Blindly Bound Screenplay Art

Project Description

Art for BMCC's Time Warner Screenwriting Fellowship during Fall 2017. This design is supposed to be a cover art for the screenplay "Blindly Bound," a dramatic feature dealing with the repercussions of physical and psycho-logical abuse throughout the generations and ending the vicious cycle.

Details

  • Adobe Illustrator CS6
  • Uses lines created with pen tool.
  • Black & white color pallete
